# Data Protection and Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 14, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Compliance:** Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019

## 1. Introduction

Crowdax API ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of our users in accordance with the Uganda Data Protection and Privacy Act, 2019 (UPDA). This policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## 2. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Consent:** You have given clear consent for processing your personal data for specific purposes
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract with you
- **Legal Obligation:** Processing is necessary for compliance with legal obligations
- **Legitimate Interest:** Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, provided these interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Personal Information

- Name and contact information (email, phone number)
- Identity verification documents (KYC)
- Financial information for investment purposes
- Consent records and preferences

### 3.2 Technical Information

- IP addresses and device information
- User agent strings
- Access logs and audit trails
- Security breach detection data

### 3.3 Business Information

- Investment history and preferences
- Campaign participation data
- Communication records

## 4. How We Use Your Data

### 4.1 Primary Purposes

- User account management and authentication
- KYC verification and compliance
- Investment processing and management
- Campaign creation and management
- Communication and support

### 4.2 Security and Compliance

- Fraud detection and prevention
- Regulatory compliance reporting
- Security breach detection and response
- Audit trail maintenance

## 5. Data Security Measures

### 5.1 Encryption

- All sensitive data is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM
- Data in transit is protected using TLS 1.3
- KYC documents are encrypted with additional security layers

### 5.2 Access Control

- Role-based access control (RBAC) implementation
- Multi-factor authentication for admin accounts
- Regular access reviews and audits

### 5.3 Breach Detection

- Real-time security monitoring
- Automated breach detection and notification
- Incident response procedures

## 6. Data Retention

### 6.1 Retention Periods

- **User Accounts:** Retained for 7 years after account closure
- **KYC Documents:** Retained for 5 years after verification
- **Transaction Records:** Retained for 7 years for regulatory compliance
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 5 years for security monitoring

### 6.2 Deletion Requests

- Users may request data deletion through our API
- Deletion requests are processed within 30 days
- Regulatory holds may prevent immediate deletion

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Right to Access

- Request a copy of your personal data
- Export your data in machine-readable format
- Access through: `GET /api/v1/users/data`

### 7.2 Right to Rectification

- Update your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Access through: `PUT /api/v1/users/data`

### 7.3 Right to Erasure

- Request deletion of your personal data
- Submit deletion requests
- Access through: `DELETE /api/v1/users/data`

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability

- Export your data in structured format
- Download your data
- Access through: `GET /api/v1/users/data/export`

### 7.5 Right to Object

- Object to processing of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Access through: `DELETE /api/v1/users/consent`

## 8. Consent Management
